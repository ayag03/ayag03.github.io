Synopsis of the film

The film "Into the Sun" shows the traveling all the way from Almaty, Kazakhstan to Abu-Dhabi,UAE. The plot starts from flying in blue clouds and ends in sunny yellow desert. That transition resembles flying into the sun a lot. Viewers can get the happy feeling of voyage after a long time of lockdowning because of 2020 circumstances.
